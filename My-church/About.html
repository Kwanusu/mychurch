Achievements

We have a school in place that meets our children's intellectual growth. We also have Soy Bible College-an affiliate of Friends Theological College Kaimosi.

Future Plans

We are looking forward to establishing a modern hospital to cater for the society's health.

Vision Statement

To reach out for the poor and less fortunate in the society

Mission Statement

It is founded on strong biblical teachings and quaker doctrine with a mission to evangelize and reach out with the gospel of Christ. With the guidance of the Missions Commission the church has a series of programmes throughout the year to reach to different places with the gospel.

History

Friends Church Huruma was established in the year 1990 by Pst Leah Temesi and others as a fellowship in her house. The number of members increased thus they had to look for a place for a church. It started as an affiliate meeting of Eldoret West Monthly Meeting of Lugari Yearly Meeting. In 1992 it was commissioned to be a monthly meeting.
